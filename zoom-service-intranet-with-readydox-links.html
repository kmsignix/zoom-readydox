Zoom Jet Packs Intranet - Mechanics

Welcome to the Zoom Jet Packs Intranet page for mechanics. Here you can find all the necessary forms for documenting inspections, services, and other maintenance activities. Please fill out and submit the forms as required.

Available Forms:

Inspection and Service Form
Inspection and Service Form
Use this form to document your inspection and service activities. Note any service actions taken, parts replaced, and any comments or observations.

Parts Inventory Request Form
Parts Inventory Request Form
Use this form to request additional parts or supplies needed for maintenance and repairs. Specify the parts required and quantities.

Incident Report Form
Incident Report Form
In the event of an incident during maintenance or operation, fill out this form to report the details. This helps in investigating and preventing future incidents.

Accident Report Form
Accident Report Form
In the event of an accident during maintenance or operation, fill out this form to report the details. This helps in investigating and preventing future accidents.

Performance Excellence Form
Performance Excellence Form
Use this form to highlight outstanding customer service or other exemplary performance by a team member. Recognizing great work improves morale and reports will be highlighted in performance reviews, spot bonuses and company meetings.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact the maintenance supervisor at maintenance@zoomjetpacks.com.
