Zoom Jet Packs Support Page

Welcome to the Zoom Jet Packs Support Page! Here you can find all the necessary forms for jet pack leasees and owners. Please fill out and sign the forms you need.

Available Forms:

Change of State and Insurance Form
Change of State and Insurance Form
Use this form to update your state of residence and insurance information. This ensures your jet pack is compliant with local regulations and covered under the appropriate insurance policies.

Additional Pilot Form
Additional Pilot Form
If you wish to authorize additional pilots to operate your jet pack, fill out this form. It includes sections for pilot information and consent.

Application Form to Join the Owners Club
Owners Club Application Form
Join the exclusive Zoom Jet Packs Owners Club! Complete this application to enjoy special benefits, events, and networking opportunities with other jet pack enthusiasts.

Consent Form to Supply Owner Information to the FAA
FAA Consent Form
This form grants permission for Zoom Jet Packs to share your ownership details with the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) as required by law.

Claim Form for Promotional Rebate
Promotional Rebate Claim Form
If you are eligible for a promotional rebate, use this form to submit your claim. Ensure you include all required documentation to process your rebate efficiently.

Accident Notification Form
Accident Notification Form
In the event of an accident involving your jet pack, fill out this form to report the incident. This helps us improve safety measures and provide necessary support.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact our support team at support@zoomjetpacks.com.
